Production

A team of visual storytellers developing and producing original multimedia content.

What is Production?

As the newest team on Scout, Production was formed to expand the Scout community and the creative services that we could provide to our clients, while also providing a space for student creators to explore and collaborate on the cool stuff that they really want to make. The Scout Production team works together on a variety of projects throughout the semester that range from client product photoshoots to marketing videos to animated short series, and anything else in between that we can figure out together and bring to life on screens.

Client Work

Production x Studio Clients: The Production team works alongside a studio team to create video, photo, and motion graphic content to support the client’s newly Scout-developed brand.

Check out our latest work with @RootedLiving on Instagram.

Production Clients: Scout Production will also open client applications for any video, photo, and motion projects in Fall 2021. Clients who have previously worked with Scout Studio are eligible to apply, as well as any other Northeastern-affiliated client with production needs.

Original Work

Scout Presents is a place for team-developed short-form projects that center around entertainment and exploration in creativity and design. Production team members collaborate to produce a slate of video, photo, audio, and motion graphic series to share through Scout Presents each semester.

Keep an eye on our Instagram and Youtube for upcoming projects.

A podcast by Scout

By Design is a podcast produced and hosted by members of Scout. Each episode, we break down impactful topics through a series of stories and conversations with our design friends. By Design offers a range of perspectives from our guests in an effort to further our collective understanding of the world of design.
